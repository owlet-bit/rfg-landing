Season Profile

Obsidian Autumn — the richest, darkest Autumn. Jet black achromatic hair against warm medium skin creates high value contrast (66pt spread). Iris saturation is intensely golden — dark hazel with amber, olive-green, and brown creating a multi-tonal warm eye. Veins read blue-green through warm skin — classic warm undertone.

Best Colors

Rich saturated warm tones that echo your dark hazel iris — deep greens that pull out the olive in your eyes, amber golds that mirror the inner ring, warm browns that ground the whole palette. Your jet black hair means you can anchor the deepest tones: evergreen, mulled wine, black walnut, deepwater teal. Warm neutrals from raven to candlelight for the foundation.

Avoid

Cool whites, icy pastels, blue-based pinks, silver, lavender, powder blue, lemon yellow, neon anything, cool greys. Pure black works — warm it with gold.
