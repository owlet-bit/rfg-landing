Season Profile

Golden Harvest — the warm heart of Autumn. Every measurement is warm (7–42°) with zero cool deviation. Hair is deep warm brown (H:37°, not black). Moderate value contrast (41–57pt spread). Iris has both amber (39°) and red-brown (10°) zones. Veins read olive-green through warm skin.

Best Colors

Colors that echo the warm brown in your hair and the amber-brown of your iris. Your warm-through-and-through coloring means the richest mid-tones are your sweet spot — aged cognac, olive grove, lion's mane, iron oxide, wild honey. Warm neutrals from heartwood to beeswax for the foundation. Nothing cool, nothing stark.

Avoid

Cool greys, navy, black (too stark — use chocolate or warm charcoal), icy blue, pink, silver, neon, cool whites. You need warmth in every piece near your face.
