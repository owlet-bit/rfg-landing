Analysis Notes

Understanding your unique color profile

Your Color Profile

You possess a fascinating chromatic complexity that defies simple seasonal classification. Your overall coloring reads as Soft Summer with significant influence from Light Summer, but there's a warm whisper threading through—visible in the golden starburst of your iris, the peachy quality of your natural flush, and how beautifully you wear soft coral and champagne tones.


Your veins read distinctly blue-purple (cool), yet your skin has warm peachy undertones when flushed. This neutral-cool with warm expression quality is what makes you so interesting to dress—and why you can successfully pull from adjacent seasons.

Your Best Qualities

  • Muted over bright—your coloring is sophisticated, not intense
  • Medium-light value range with ability to go deeper for drama
  • Cool-neutral undertone with warm expression capacity
  • High contrast between hair depth and skin (especially as blonde)
  • Eye colors that shift between sage, moss, and grey-green

Colors to Approach with Caution

  • Pure bright white (use soft white or ivory instead)
  • Stark black (charcoal and soft navy are better)
  • Neon or highly saturated colors
  • Very warm oranges and yellows
  • Olive greens with too much yellow

Power Combinations

  • Teal Velvet + Champagne Blush + Gold jewelry
  • Dusty Rose + Soft Navy + Silver accents
  • Sage Mist + Cocoa Grey + Cream
  • Deep Teal + Pearl Pink (evening glamour)
  • Soft Coral + Stone Blue + Taupe
