Four laws

Patina over glareChoose velvet, washed silk, matte crepe, suede, brushed metal, irregular pearl, and softly returning light.
Depth is structuralWarm Ink, Forest Ink, Deep Petrol, and Aubergine can anchor a long column without becoming harsh.
The middle must earn entryA midtone works through kinship, useful chroma, or attachment to a pale or deep pole—not as undifferentiated fog.
Curve carries the colorFit the bust, locate the natural waist, skim the hip, and continue the eye downward.
