Your Color DNA

A scientific analysis of your unique natural coloring reveals a harmonious blend of cool and neutral undertones with the subtle warmth of sage and gold.

Hair Color

Multi-dimensional ash blonde with cool brown roots transitioning to wheat and champagne. The natural variation creates a soft, diffused effect that harmonizes with muted, blended colors rather than stark contrasts.

Skin Tone

Fair with translucent quality and pink-peachy undertones. Your skin reads neutral-cool with visible warmth in the flush areas. This delicate balance means you can wear both soft warm and soft cool colors, but should avoid high contrast or overly bright tones.

Vein Color

Blue-violet dominant with some blue-green present, indicating cool to neutral undertones. This confirms your ability to wear the muted, cool-influenced colors of the Soft Summer palette.

Flush Color

True rose-pink that reads cool rather than coral. Your natural flush is your color compass—it tells us you harmonize with rose, mauve, and pink-based colors over orange or peach-based warmth.

Lip Color

Dusty rose with mauve undertones—soft, muted, and elegant. Your natural lip color is a perfect template for your best makeup shades: nothing too bright, nothing too warm, always softened.

Eye Color

Extraordinary hazel-green with complex depth. A warm golden-amber starburst radiates from the pupil, transitioning through sage green and teal to a grayish-blue outer ring. These eyes contain your "magic colors"—the specific shades that will make your eyes luminous. Sage green, muted teal, warm gold, and soft blue-gray all echo directly in your iris.

Makeup Palette

Colors formulated to enhance your natural coloring without overwhelming your soft, luminous complexion.

Lip Colors

Your best lip colors are dusty roses, mauves, and soft berries. Avoid anything too bright or too orange. For everyday, a sheer rose balm; for evening, a deeper mauve or soft burgundy.

Blush

Soft pink and rose blushes mimic your natural flush. Apply with a light hand—your skin's translucency means a little goes a long way.

Eye Shadow

Sage, taupe, soft plum, and rose gold bring out the green and gold in your eyes. Avoid black—use soft charcoal or deep plum for definition instead.

Bronzer & Highlight

Soft, cool-toned bronzers with a hint of rose prevent you from looking muddy. For highlight, champagne and soft pink give a natural glow.
