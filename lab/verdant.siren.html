02

Your Star Colors

The colors that make your unique features sing

Deep Teal #2D7580

Why it works: This is your eye color amplified. Deep teal echoes that striking dark outer ring around your iris, making the whole eye appear larger and more luminous while the gold ring pops against it.

Blue-Forest #3A7868

Why it works: This cool, blue-leaning forest green sits between your teal outer ring and sage mid-zone, creating seamless harmony. Your eyes become the focal point without competing contrast.

Burgundy #7A2028

Why it works: Deep burgundy creates magnetic contrast with your green eyes (red-green complementary magic) while the cool, blue-based red harmonizes with your skin's pink undertones and natural flush.

Plum #6B4880

Why it works: Purple contains both the red that contrasts your green eyes and the blue that harmonizes with your cool undertones. This depth of plum adds drama while flattering your translucent skin.

Raspberry #B85565

Why it works: This cool pink-red picks up your natural flush and creates vibrant contrast with your green-gold eyes. It reads as healthy and magnetic against your cool-pink undertones.

Navy #1E3A5F

Why it works: Deep navy provides a sophisticated neutral that enhances your cool undertones while the blue relates to the teal in your outer iris ring. Creates beautiful framing for your face.

Sage #8A9B78

Why it works: Sage directly matches the mid-zone of your iris, creating effortless harmony. This is your "easy" color - it makes your eyes the star while keeping the overall effect soft and approachable.

Dusty Rose #D4A0A8

Why it works: This soft, muted rose relates directly to your natural flush and lip color, creating a harmonious glow. The dusty quality keeps it sophisticated rather than saccharine against your cool skin.
